    Blue Team accounts

    There are a few ways to do blue team accounts. One way is to this is that each team has one account. This will be shared accout for all Team members. The best way to do this would be to set the username to something like siteN. Where N is the site number.

    The other method of doing Blue Team account is to have an account for each Team member. This approch allows for better accountabily on the teams side to see who did what.
